*1. Which factors appear to influence subscription renewal the most?*

User engagement factors such as weekly watch hours, subscription plans, monthly spending, and device usage seem to have the strongest impact on subscription renewal. Active users are more likely to continue their subscriptions.


*2. Why is subscription renewal a classification problem?*

Subscription renewal is considered a classification problem because the prediction is divided into categories like “Renewed” or “Not Renewed.” The model predicts a specific class instead of a numerical value.


*3. Why is monthly spending a regression problem?*

Monthly spending is a regression problem because the output is a numeric amount that can vary continuously. Regression models are used to predict such continuous values.
